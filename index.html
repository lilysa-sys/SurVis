2.2 ART in Complex and Intelligent Systems

Paper 4: ARTDL: Adaptive Random Testing for Deep Learning Systems

DOI: 10.1109/ACCESS.2019.2962695

This paper adapts ART for deep learning systems, introducing techniques to focus on regions of the input space that are more likely to induce failures, making it a crucial tool for testing AI models.

Paper 5: Efficient and Unbiased Safety Test for Autonomous Driving Systems

DOI: 10.1109/TIV.2022.3213310

Explores an unbiased safety testing strategy for autonomous driving systems that combines simulation with real-world testing to ensure diverse scenario coverage without redundancy.

Paper 6: Adaptive random testing of mobile applications

DOI: 10.1109/ICCET.2010.5485442

This paper presents the application of ART in mobile applications, emphasizing dynamic input adjustments to handle diverse device configurations and user behaviors.

2.3 ART Hybridization and Evolution

Paper 7: A novel evolutionary approach for adaptive random testing

DOI: 10.1109/TR.2009.2034288

This paper introduces an evolutionary ART approach, combining ART with evolutionary algorithms to improve test coverage in high-dimensional input spaces.

Paper 8: Adaptive stress testing for autonomous vehicles

DOI: 10.1109/IVS.2018.8500400

This study proposes Adaptive Stress Testing (AST) using reinforcement learning to discover low-probability high-risk scenarios, particularly for autonomous vehicles.

Paper 9: Accelerated Testing and Evaluation for Black-Box Autonomous Driving Systems via Adaptive Markov Chain Monte Carlo

DOI: 10.1109/TITS.2024.3525059

Proposes an accelerated testing method based on MCMC, guiding the sampling process toward high-risk areas, improving testing efficiency for black-box autonomous systems.

2.4 ART and Testing Paradigm Extensions

Paper 10: A Survey on Metamorphic Testing

DOI: 10.1109/TSE.2016.2532875

This paper introduces metamorphic testing (MT), a testing method used when traditional oracles are not available, and shows how ART and MT can be combined for more comprehensive testing.
